Easy Coach Bus Fares and Prices

The fares and bus prices for Easy Coach ranges between Ksh 1,000 and Ksh 2,000. These prices differ depending on the destination and also the travel season. Buses from Nairobi to Busia costs Ksh 1,500, while the ones from Nairobi to Homabay cost Ksh 1,000. The fares increase during festive seasons, holidays, and back-to-school seasons.
